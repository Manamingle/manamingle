Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

1. Absolute No-Data Storage Policy

Mana Mingle is built with a zero data storage architecture. We do not save, store, or retain any user information — ever.

  • ❌No chat messages are stored
  • ❌No call data is recorded
  • ❌No usernames, emails, or profiles
  • ❌No IP addresses permanently logged
  • ❌No tracking or analytics cookies
  • ❌No database exists
  • ❌No long-term identifiers

2. Temporary & In-Memory Operation

All system data exists only in server memory while connected.

  • ✅Data lives only during active session
  • ✅Disconnecting deletes everything
  • ✅Server restarts wipe all data
  • ✅No recovery after disconnect

3. Anonymous Identity System

Users receive a temporary random ID for moderation only.

  • ✅Session-only random IDs
  • ❌No real-world identity link
  • ❌IDs cannot be tracked
  • ❌IDs vanish on exit

4. Coins, VIP & Features

Virtual coins and premium features are non-persistent.

  • ✅Coins exist per session only
  • ✅Coins reset on refresh
  • ❌No balances saved
  • ❌No payment data stored

5. WebRTC & Communication

  • ✅Peer-to-peer WebRTC
  • ❌No audio or video recording
  • ❌No call metadata storage

6. GDPR, DPDP & CCPA

Since no personal data is stored, most legal obligations do not apply.

  • Right to Access: No data exists
  • Right to Erasure: Automatic
  • Right to Portability: Not applicable
  • DPO Requirement: Not required

7. Children’s Privacy

Mana Mingle is strictly for 18+ users. Minors are not permitted.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use means acceptance of the latest version.
